This is the input image folder.

------------------
SUBJECT IMAGES
------------------

You must add MRA images from CASILab*, following the name convention
Normal###-MRA.nii

where ### is an increasing number

* CASILab at The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and distributed by the MIDAS Data Server at Kitware, Inc.
https://www.insight-journal.org/midas/community/view/21
